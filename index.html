HOW TO PLAY?

Help 1Q800 to be ready for all the challenges! Drag and drop 7 light sources into the 1Q800 in the correct sequence to move on to the next level.

How to Score?

+20 points when light sources are selected in the right order.

-2 points when incorrect light sources are selected.
